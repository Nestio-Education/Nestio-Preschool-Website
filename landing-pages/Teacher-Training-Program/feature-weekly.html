How It Works

Structure of Each Week

01

Clear Learning Objectives

Every week starts with 3 measurable outcomes — so trainees know exactly what they'll be able to do by Friday.

02

Daily Topic + Activity

Monday to Friday, each day has a focused topic and a hands-on activity — no passive lectures, only active learning.

03

Theory Linked to Practice

Every activity is explicitly connected to a learning theory or curriculum framework — bridging the gap between what and why.

04

Weekly Reflection

Every Friday closes with a review, peer discussion, or reflective journal entry to consolidate learning.
