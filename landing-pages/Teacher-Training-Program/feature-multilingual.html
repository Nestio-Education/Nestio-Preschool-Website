Why It Matters

Mother Tongue First

Research consistently shows that children learn best when instruction happens in their mother tongue. NEP 2020 mandates multilingual education — SpacECE has built the resources to make it a reality.

Our multilingual curriculum ensures that teachers from Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Hindi-speaking states have the same high-quality materials — not a translation afterthought, but a purpose-built resource in their own language.
