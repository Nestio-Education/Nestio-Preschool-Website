Family Engagement Strategies

Parent-Teacher Conferences

Trainees learn how to conduct meaningful, empathetic parent meetings — sharing progress clearly and handling sensitive conversations with care.

Parent Capacity Building

Dedicated workshop materials that help parents understand child development, create stimulating home environments, and extend learning beyond the classroom.

Activity Calendars & Newsletters

Monthly family newsletters and home activity calendars that keep parents involved, informed, and excited about their child's learning journey.

Parent Awareness Videos

A YouTube playlist that explains the WHY behind classroom activities — perfect to share at PTMs or during parent orientation sessions.

Home Learning Extensions

Suggested follow-up activities parents can do at home — connecting school learning to everyday family life in simple, playful ways.

Community Engagement

Strategies for involving the wider community — local helpers, cultural events, and neighbourhood resources that enrich the classroom experience.

The Home–School Connection

Research shows that children whose parents are actively engaged in their education achieve better outcomes across every developmental domain. SpacECE trains teachers to be the bridge — not just between child and curriculum, but between school and family.
