Where Health & Safety Fits

Month 5 · Week 19

Child Health, Safety & Nutrition is a dedicated full week within Month 5 of the SpacECE Training Programme — covering everything from first aid to nutrition planning to safeguarding protocols.

Child Protection & Safeguarding

Every teacher must be the first line of protection for the children in their care. This module covers recognition, reporting, and response.

Recognise signs of abuse and neglect
Understand mandatory reporting duty
Apply POCSO Act guidelines in school
Create a safe, trusting classroom environment

Emergency Preparedness & First Aid

Teachers are often the first adult on scene when a child is hurt. Learn to respond calmly and correctly in the most common preschool emergencies.

Basic first aid: cuts, burns, choking
Emergency evacuation procedures
When and how to call for medical help
Incident documentation and reporting

Hygiene & Healthy Habits

Young children learn lifelong hygiene habits in the preschool years. Teachers are the key to establishing routines that stick.

Teach handwashing through songs & games
Classroom sanitisation routines
Toilet training support strategies
Illness prevention and management

Nutrition Planning

Good nutrition is the foundation of learning. Teachers learn to plan age-appropriate snacks, spot nutritional deficiencies, and engage families.

Age-appropriate meal and snack planning
Signs of malnutrition in young children
Food allergy awareness and management
Linking nutrition to developmental outcomes
