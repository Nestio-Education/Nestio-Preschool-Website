Who We Are

About SpacECE India Foundation

🏆 NEP 2020 Aligned

Our Vision

To create a nurturing educational ecosystem where every teacher is equipped to foster holistic development in young children, building the foundation for India's future.

Our Mission

Empowering educators through accessible, high-quality, multilingual training programs grounded in Indian educational values and modern evidence-based pedagogy.

NEP 2020 Aligned Curriculum

Our curriculum emphasises experiential learning, critical thinking, multilingualism, and play-based approaches — fully aligned with the National Education Policy 2020 and NIPUN Bharat guidelines.

Learning Objectives

Defining clear, measurable goals for every lesson aligned with developmental milestones.

SMART Objectives

Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound goals for early learners.

Bloom's Taxonomy

Structuring questions and tasks from remembering to creating — for all ability levels.

Teaching Methods

Play-way, Montessori, inquiry-based, and project-based learning strategies.

Interactive Learning

Assessment Techniques

Formative and summative approaches: observation, portfolios, and checklists.

Cross-Curricular Links

Weaving literacy, numeracy, arts and STEM together through thematic planning.

Reflective Planning

Reviewing and adapting plans based on children's responses and observations.

Field Work Components

Practicum Guidelines

Rules, expectations, and conduct guidelines for all school observation visits.

Observation Reports

Structured templates for observing and documenting classroom dynamics.

Reflection Journals

Daily reflective prompts to support continuous professional growth.

Classroom Visits

Scheduled visits to partner preschools — shadow, assist, and then lead teaching.

Mentor Feedback Sessions

Structured one-to-one feedback from experienced ECE mentors.

Micro-Teaching Practice

Practice teaching with peers in simulated classroom sessions before the real practicum.
