What's Included

Field Work Components

Practicum Guidelines

Clear rules, expectations, and conduct guidelines for all school observation visits — so trainees are prepared and professional from day one.

Observation Reports

Structured templates for observing and documenting classroom dynamics — teaching style, child behaviour, environment, and transitions.

Reflection Journals

Daily reflective prompts to help trainees process their experiences, identify growth areas, and build a habit of reflective practice.

Classroom Visits

Scheduled visits to partner preschools — shadow first, then assist, then lead. A carefully sequenced progression to classroom confidence.

Mentor Feedback

Structured one-to-one feedback sessions from experienced ECE mentors using a standardised rubric for fair, constructive evaluation.

Micro-Teaching

Practice teaching with peers in safe, simulated classroom sessions before entering a real preschool — build confidence without pressure.
