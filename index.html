Welcome to Nestio

An innovative early childhood program that goes beyond traditional preschool learning.

Nestio Preschool is an innovative early childhood program by SpacECE, led by experienced Early Childhood Education experts — designed to go beyond traditional preschool learning through structured, station-based exploration.

We believe every child is born curious. At Nestio, we nurture that curiosity where specialist educators lead children through Sensory, Literacy, Numeracy, and Arts experiences every single day.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the most trusted early childhood brand, building bright foundations for every learner.

🚀 Our Mission

Deliver joyful, research-led, station-based education through expert educators.

How Learning Happens at Nestio

Educators lead their area of expertise as children move across stations for enriched learning experiences.

🖐️

Sensory Station

Hands-on experiences sparking thinking and creativity. Children explore textures, sounds, smells, and materials in a rich sensory environment.

📖

Literacy Station

Building language, reading readiness, and communication through storytelling, phonics, and vocabulary-rich activities.

🔢

Numeracy Station

Early maths through patterns, counting, shapes, and problem-solving. Making numbers fun and meaningful for young minds.

🎨

Arts Station

Creative expression through drawing, painting, craft and collage. Building fine motor skills and artistic confidence daily.
