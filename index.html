Welcome to Nestio

An innovative early childhood program that goes beyond traditional preschool learning.

Nestio Preschool is an innovative early childhood program by SpacECE, led by experienced Early Childhood Education experts — designed to go beyond traditional preschool learning through structured, station-based exploration.

We believe every child is born curious. At Nestio, we nurture that curiosity where specialist educators lead children through Sensory, Literacy, Numeracy, and Arts experiences every single day.

Specialized Teaching, Structured Exploration

How Learning Happens at Nestio

Educators lead their area of expertise as children move across stations for enriched learning experiences.

Sensory Station

Hands-on experiences sparking thinking and creativity. Children explore textures, sounds, smells, and materials in a rich sensory environment.

Literacy Station

Building language, reading readiness, and communication through storytelling, phonics, and vocabulary-rich activities.

Numeracy Station

Early maths through patterns, counting, shapes, and problem-solving. Making numbers fun and meaningful for young minds.

Arts Station

Creative expression through drawing, painting, craft and collage. Building fine motor skills and artistic confidence daily.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling your child at Nestio Preschool.

Children aged 2–3 years are eligible for Playgroup (Curious Minds), and children aged 3–4 years for Nursery (Growing Stars). We also offer a Parent–Toddler Program for children aged 1–2 years.
Book a free visit to the school, meet our team, and fill the admission form. Required documents typically include the child's birth certificate, passport-size photos, parent/guardian ID proof, and address proof. Our team will guide you through every step.
Regular school hours are Mon–Sat: 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM. We currently offer a half-day program designed for young learners. During the Settling & Transition Program (15–30 June), hours are 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM.
We maintain a low and healthy teacher–student ratio to ensure every child receives personalized attention. Each learning station is led by a specialist educator, ensuring focused, enriched engagement at all times.
Yes! All Nestio educators are trained through SpacECE's 17+ year expertise in Early Childhood Education. Each station has a domain specialist — not a generalist — so children benefit from deep expertise in sensory, literacy, numeracy, and arts learning.
Nestio follows a research-led, station-based curriculum developed by SpacECE ECE specialists. It integrates the best of play-way, experiential, and structured learning — children rotate through Sensory, Literacy, Numeracy, and Arts stations every day.
Activities include sensory exploration, storytelling and phonics, early numeracy with patterns and shapes, arts and crafts, music, dance, drama, 8+ structured sports (Yoga, Football, Basketball, Martial Arts, Swimming, Archery, Athletics, Rope Yoga), and nature-based discovery play.
Child safety is our top priority. The school maintains strict supervision at all times, follows our published Child Safety Policy, and ensures no child is ever left unsupervised — whether in class, during play, meals, or bathroom breaks. All staff are background-verified.
Nestio maintains high standards of cleanliness — classrooms, restrooms, and play areas are sanitized daily. Children are guided in regular handwashing routines, and hygiene practices are woven into daily activities to build healthy habits from an early age.
We have a Caretaker & Medical Emergency Protocol in place. Parents fill out a Medical Emergency Form at the time of joining (link in our Updates section). In case of any emergency, parents are notified immediately and the child is given first aid while medical assistance is arranged.
Yes! Nestio has a dedicated outdoor play area where children enjoy free play, structured sports, and nature exploration. Our 8+ sports program is conducted by trained female coaches and includes activities for full physical development.
There is a dedicated Snack & Social Time each day (part of our daily schedule). Parents are encouraged to send a healthy home-cooked snack for their child. We guide children in healthy eating habits and make mealtimes a warm, social experience.
Parents receive updates through our school app (included in the fee), WhatsApp group communications, Parent–Teacher Meetings (PTMs), and our newsletter. We believe in transparent, regular communication to keep parents actively involved in their child's growth.
We have a dedicated Settling & Transition Program (June 15–30) where parents can accompany their child. Our teachers are trained to handle separation anxiety with warmth, patience, and age-appropriate activities. The gradual settling approach helps children feel safe, happy, and loved from day one. 🌱
